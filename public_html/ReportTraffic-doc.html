Incoming/Outgoing Network Traffic - Description

This graph shows the IP traffic seen flowing by your network connection(s) that is travelling between local and remote computers (traffic observed travelling from local to local or from remote to remote is shown on the Internal/External Traffic graph). Packets which are sent from remote to local machines are plotted as Incoming - packets travelling in the other direction are Outgoing.

Local computers are those whose IP address falls in LOCALRANGE as specified in the ipaudit-web.conf configuration file.

The traffic is sampled over 30 minute intervals.
